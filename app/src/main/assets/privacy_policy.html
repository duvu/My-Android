Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through the My Android application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our App.

Information We Collect

Our App accesses certain information from your device to provide its core functionality. This includes:

  • Device Information: We access but do not store or transmit information such as your device model, manufacturer, Android version, screen size, and memory usage.
  • Applications List: We access the list of installed applications on your device to display them to you but do not store or transmit this information.
  • Call Logs: With your explicit permission, we access your call history to display it within the App but do not store or transmit this information.
  • Storage Information: We access information about your device's storage to display memory usage statistics but do not store or transmit this information.

How We Use Your Information

All information accessed by the App is processed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers or any third parties, with the following exceptions:

  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect information as described in the "Third-Party Services" section below.

Third-Party Services

Our App includes the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain information to provide personalized ads. For more information about how Google AdMob collects and processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Choices and Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Permissions: You can manage the permissions granted to the App through your device's settings at any time.
  • Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's settings (Settings > Google > Ads) or through the App's settings.
  • Data Access and Deletion: Since we do not store your data on our servers, there is no need for data access or deletion requests.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information accessed by our App. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Specific Regional Rights

If you are located in the EEA, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. For Brazil Residents - LGPD

If you are located in Brazil, you have certain rights under the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD), similar to those provided under the GDPR.
